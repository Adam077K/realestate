## Acquisition Funnel (AARRR)

> **AARRR Metric Definitions** — define each stage specifically for your product before filling in numbers:
> - **Acquisition**: First meaningful touch (not just a pageview). e.g., "Signs up via email" or "Installs the app."
> - **Activation**: User reaches the "Aha moment" — the first time they experience core value. _Define your Aha moment here:_ `_[e.g., "User creates their first X and sees the result"]_`
> - **Retention**: User returns and performs a core action more than once within the retention window.
> - **Revenue**: User converts to a paid plan or completes a monetizable action.
> - **Referral**: User intentionally shares the product with someone else (invite, share link, word of mouth).
>
> Industry benchmarks: Activation >40%, D7 Retention >20% (consumer) / >40% (B2B SaaS), Paid Conversion 2–5% (freemium).

## Viral & Referral Metrics

> **K-factor formula:** `K = (Invites sent per active user) × (Conversion rate of those invites)`
> K > 1.0 = viral loop (product grows on its own). K 0.1–0.5 = meaningful organic component. K < 0.1 = essentially zero viral.
